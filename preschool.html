Sussex Martlets Preschool Trampolining runs two sessions on a Thursday and accommodates children from the age of two to four years old.




Preschool Trampolining (term times)

Thursdays                             1:00pm to 1:45pm                           2 to 4 years
Thursdays                             1:45pm to 2:30pm                           2 to 4 years

Sessions are paid on a pay as you go system. Preschool children will have fun learning to jump on the trampoline and learn some basic trampoline skills.
