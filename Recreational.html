Sussex Martlets run two Recreational Trampolining sessions a week for children from the age of five upwards.


Recreational Sessions (term times)

Mondays                             4:30pm to 5:30pm                            5 years+
Fridays                                4:30pm to 5:30pm                            5 years+

Sessions are paid for each half term. Children learn basic trampoline skills and work towards the British Gymnastics Trampoline Proficiency Award Scheme.
